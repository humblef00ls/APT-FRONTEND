APT: Portmanteau

filename: PortManteau.py def combine(first,flen,second,slen): """ return string that's a portmanteau of strings first and second using flen chars from first and slen chars from second """ # you write code here return ""

Problem Statement

A portmanteau is a word made by combining parts of other words. Examples include brunch (from breakfast and lunch), jeggings (from jeans and leggings), guesstimate (from guess and estimate) and digerati (from digital and literati).

Write a function combine that returns a portmanteau from two words, first and second that are parameters to the function. Combine the first flen characters of first with the last slen characters of second. See the examples for details.

Constraints

  • first and second will each contain between 1 and 250 characters.

  • flen will be positive and less than or equal to the length of first

  • slen will be positive and less than or equal to the length of second
